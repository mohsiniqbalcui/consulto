Office
Environment

Office design is a major consideration for a new small business owner. Some companies select a basic open departmental design, with groups of employees in various areas of the office (no walls), while others prefer a more private cubicle format. The choice of office design depends on a number of factors, including the noise level of the office, office culture, space available, and the specific needs and duties of employees. For instance, if you employ sales professionals or call center workers, barriers created by cubicles can help prevent conversations from crossing.

Make sure that the office space you lease is sufficient to house all of your employees comfortably. Many localities also have strict rules about the maximum number of occupants for a building or space, so check with your local town office or potential lessor before signing paperwork.
